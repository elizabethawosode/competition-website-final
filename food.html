Top 10 Christmas Cake Ideas

The festive season is right around the corner, and we’re sure you’re dreaming of all the tasty treats you and your family can enjoy together. Of course, wonderful homemade, decorated cakes not only offer a tempting treat to share at holiday parties, but also always make fantastic gifts for neighbors, family and friends with a ravenous sweet tooth.

Candy Cane Forest Cake

Twirl up a white cake covered with crushed peppermints and candy canes

Stained Glass Cake

Melted Jolly Ranchers become the perfect "panes" on this cake.

Candied Pretzel Cake

This scrumptious cake delivers the best of both worlds: sweet and salty.

Pine Tree Forest Cake

Create different sizes and colors of these sweet trees for a simple yet impressive display.

Christmas Tree Sheet Cake

Ornamental gumdrops, M and M’s, and starlight mints take shape around a caramel corn garland and a chocolate candy base. Colorful sprinkles in the cake batter give this cake a second helping of whimsy.

Gingerbread Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting

Top off a simple cake with a gingerbread house for a creative, festive finish

Cranberry, Orange, and Walnut Layer Cake

Embrace cranberry season by crowning this cake with fresh, sugared berries.

Chocolate Coconut Winter Wonderland Cake

Who says Christmas cakes can only be red and green? The pastel hues of the Pocky stick-and-melted-chocolate trees topping this dessert reinforce the white winter wonderland theme.g

Chocolate Pine Cone Cake

Or, if you prefer a more rustic look, try this nature-inspired chocolate creation.

Chocolate Peppermint Crunch Bundt Cake

Refreshing peppermint candies are the perfect complement to rich, decadent chocolate cake.

5 Unique Christmas Turkey Recipies

Christmas wouldn’t be complete without a whole roast turkey. From the traditional roast to more modern versions, we've got the perfect turkey recipe for you. If you’re worried about cooking the perfect Christmas turkey because you’re afraid you’ll get it wrong, don’t be.

Red Chile and Orange Glazed Turkey

This turkey, complete with dried chile peppers and orange zest, will heat up your holiday.

Turkey Roulade 3 Ways

Turkey roulade is a new way to step up your turkey game. 

Easiest Salt-and-Pepper Turkey

This holiday recipe may be simple, but it's sure to be a crowd pleaser.

Pear-Thyme Brined Turkey

Be sure to choose a fresh, unbrined turkey for this recipe. Many varieties are injected with a salt solution prior to packaging and will make this dish too salty. Reserve a couple of thyme springs to use in the gravy.

Fried Turkey

If you have a crowd or want extra leftovers, you can fry an additional turkey in the same oil—just be sure to heat it back up to 350 degrees F. Save your oil containers; they'll come in handy when it's time to clean up.

The Perfect Side Dish

Fill your table with these delicious sides this holiday season. Some say side dishes are the true stars of the holiday table. Complete your meal with a selection of sweet and savory sides.

Buttermilk and Hatch Chile Grits

For extra presentation points, serve these creamy grits in Mason jars.

Braised Greens

Add some flavor to these dark, leafy veggies with garlic, onion, and loads of crispy bacon.

Skillet Cornbread

Serve this buttery cornbread straight from the skillet for a rustic vibe.

Cheesy Rice

Add onions, bell peppers, and a big helping of Velveeta cheese to make the ultimate side dish.

Texas-Style Pimento Cheese

Serve this cheese spread alongside Masa Biscuits for a flavor-packed combo.
